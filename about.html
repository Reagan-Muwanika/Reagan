About US

At the core of our business model is quality management,
coordinating wide-scale projects across the nation. Our use of unique
database management software allows us to deliver results to our
clients in real-time.

We provide logistics and execute equipment removals for major
telecommunications carriers and their partners. This includes, but is
not limited to, equipment deinstallation and cable mining, including
BTS, switch, RF and power cable, PBU, battery, ancillary materials
extraction and removal services from cell and switch sites.

Mure Ventures' expertise in process management, documentation,
safety, enforcement, security, and overall systemic coverage amongst
its management teams raises the bar in terms of quality control in the
industry.

Highly Trained Personnel

Our in-house crews have comprehensive training in both the
technical aspect of our work as well as in the importance of safety
awareness. Certifications obtained by our employees include:

  • OSHA 10 Hour Course
  • OSHA 30 Hour Course
  • RF Safety Awareness course
  • CPR/First Aid Training
  • Verizon Air 21-32 Swap Course

Safety

We hold our employees and crew members to the highest standards
and best practices, as established by Federal and State laws, industry
certification and oversight groups, and company policies. We
reinforce the importance of upholding the highest level of safety
awareness and hazard recognition on work sites. We have
implemented a system of policy communication, awareness training,
continuing education, inspections, audits, and proper documentation
to ensure full compliance with our company's high standards.
